My Website Journey

  1. Home
  2. Posts
  3. My website journey
#webdev #hugo #journey

Hey there!, Adit’s here, and this is my first post. I want to share a story of my journey in creating this site, my portfolio website.

My first journey in creating a website started 1 years ago, middle 2021. I started with wordpress, I bought my first domain, bytadit.com (which is expired now), as well as bought my first hosting package at idcloudhost. It’s quite memorial to me, since i spent almost 60 bucks from my own savings for the things that i want to do, but i don’t know how to do. Seriously, at that time, I didn’t know how to use html, css, or even js at all. My motivation at that time was just feeling cool of having your own site, with your own custom domain. But of course, i have other motivations especially for learning something new. I learned a lot of new things like cpanel, phpmyadmin, and wordpress of course. Honestly, I am a very neat person, love beauty, and originality, and i want to implement these on my site. However, since at that time I didn’t know anything about css, in the end I only used elementor in setting up my site, and we all know that website customization with elementor, especially free ones, is very limited and it’s very unsatisfactory, like it or not i need to study html, css. Finally I tried to learn html, css, even js, and also tried to find other alternatives besides wordpress, since i felt that my site were running slow at wordpress. I’m also trying to find a free hosting alternative, since I felt burdened with monthly hosting fees. I finally met Static Site Generator (SSG) Hugo , as well as Netlify and that’s where my journey on static sites started.

Hugo taught me the concept of modularity which is similar to MVC(i learned now in laravel), while netlify taught me how to manage repositories, and that’s when I first learned to use git, and tried the github and gitlab platforms. Since using SSG, my problem solving skills increased significantly. I have encountered many problems but solved them at the same time. Like a young person, I get bored easily and want to try new things, since then I have rebuilt my site many times. I have tried dozens of hugo themes and customized them, but I am never satisfied. Finally I understand what I need is originality. With enough knowledge about web dev (html, css, js, and even tailwind library, jquery) i learned, now i finally decided to make this theme from scratch (I named it ideal theme, since i think it’s ideal to me). Since the old domain (bytadit.com) has been forfeited, as a result I didn’t pay it (I didn’t have the money at that time), i decided to bought a new domain that i use now, bytadit.me. I bought on NameCheap, for free, because I’m using the Github Student Developer Pack, which gives you a free (.me ) domain for 1 year. I hope that this domain (bytadit.me) will last a long time, and the theme (ideal theme) that I created my own, will grow and maintain its originality.
